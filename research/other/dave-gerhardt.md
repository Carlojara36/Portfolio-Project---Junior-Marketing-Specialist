# Dave Gerhardt — Additional Sources

## Podcast: Exit Five #7
- **Title:** Building a B2B Marketing Strategy That Will Actually Work
- **URL:** https://www.exitfive.com/podcast/building-a-b2b-marketing-strategy-that-will-work
- **Core insight:** Foundational solo episode. Explains his **marketing planning framework**: start with making sales easier, work backwards. Simple strategy framework (goals → strategies → tactics) that became the organizing principle of Exit Five.

## Podcast: Exit Five #214
- **Title:** Q&A with Dave: Brand vs. Demand, Personality In Your Marketing
- **URL:** https://www.exitfive.com/podcast/q-a-with-dave-brand-vs-demand-personality-in-your-marketing-and-the-most-successful-b2b-marketing-campaigns
- **Core insight:** How to balance brand vs. demand, inject personality into B2B marketing, structure a marketing team. Introduces **"marketable moments"** concept and differentiation in crowded markets.

## Podcast: Exit Five #1
- **Title:** How to Think About Strategy in B2B Marketing
- **URL:** https://www.exitfive.com/podcast/how-to-think-about-strategy-in-b2b-marketing
- **Core insight:** Literally the first episode (originally "B2B Marketing Micro-Lesson"). Establishes the difference between goals, strategies, and tactics — the core mental model underpinning all of Exit Five's content.
