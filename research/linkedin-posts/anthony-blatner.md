## Post 1: LinkedIn Is the #1 AI-Cited Domain for Business
- **Date:** 2026
- **Summary:** Anthony shares data that LinkedIn is the #1 cited domain for professional queries across all 6 major AI platforms. It appears in 11% of all AI responses — ahead of Wikipedia, YouTube, and major news publishers.
- **Key Takeaway:** LinkedIn content now influences AI answers. Optimizing LinkedIn posts for LLM citation is a new SEO frontier.

## Post 2: Thought Leader Ads vs Sponsored Content
- **Date:** 2025
- **Summary:** Anthony explains that Thought Leader Ads generate 3-5x higher click-through rates than standard sponsored content. People are on LinkedIn to hear from people, not ads from companies.
- **Key Takeaway:** Amplify people, not company pages. Thought Leader Ads are the highest-ROI LinkedIn ad format.

## Post 3: Don't Use Default Settings
- **Date:** 2025 (Exit Five Podcast)
- **Summary:** Anthony warns that LinkedIn's default ad settings include "audience expansion" that shows your ads to people outside your target. On LinkedIn, where B2B targeting precision is the value, this wastes budget.
- **Key Takeaway:** Turn off audience expansion. Tight targeting beats broad reach on LinkedIn.

## Post 4: Cost Per Click Benchmarks
- **Date:** 2025
- **Summary:** Anthony shares benchmarks: average LinkedIn click costs $10-15 for standard company page ads. Thought Leader Ads with high engagement get $1-2 per click. The platform rewards content that keeps users engaged.
- **Key Takeaway:** High engagement = lower costs. The ad platform wants to deliver content users enjoy.

## Post 5: Retargeting Strategy
- **Date:** 2025
- **Summary:** Anthony recommends starting with website retargeting (pixel your site) and company page visitor retargeting. Company pages rank well in Google, so you likely already have warm traffic to retarget.
- **Key Takeaway:** Start with warm audiences. Don't go cold until you've maximized retargeting.
