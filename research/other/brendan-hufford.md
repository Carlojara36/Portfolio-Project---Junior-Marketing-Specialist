# Brendan Hufford — Additional Sources

## Podcast: Marketing Powerups
- **Title:** Brendan Hufford's Content IP Framework
- **URL:** https://www.marketingpowerups.com/podcast/brendan-huffords-content-ip-framework/
- **Core insight:** Definitive episode on the **Content IP Framework** — his core methodology. Explains why keyword research is dead for demand gen, how naming customer problems drives $134K+/month in traffic. Introduces "checkbox marketing" and "relay race marketing" concepts.

## Podcast: Content Disrupted
- **Title:** Growth Sprints: Brendan Hufford on What's Killing Your Content Marketing
- **URL:** https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/growth-sprints-brendan-hufford-on-whats-killing-your/id1708373670?i=1000708593566
- **Core insight:** Deep dive on why most content marketing is broken, how to escape "checkbox marketing." Introduces the **"immortal newsletter"** concept — 50% evergreen nurture + 50% ephemeral content.

## Article: Content IP Framework
- **URL:** https://growthsprints.co/content-ip/
- **Core insight:** Brendan's own written explanation. Codifies the methodology: name a problem customers face (not a category for your solution), create IP around that named problem, drive pipeline through demand-focused content.
