## Post 1: 4-Step Content Production System
- **Date:** March 2022
- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/justinwelsh_every-saturday-i-create-20-linkedin-posts-activity-6911646251151192064-qpv3
- **Engagement:** 387+ comments
- **Summary:** Justin shares his system for creating 20 LinkedIn posts in one hour on Saturdays. The 4-step process: Curate → Templatize → Rapidly create → Distribute + Boost. He emphasizes never staring at a blank screen and having everything automated like an assembly line.
- **Key Takeaway:** Batch creation is the secret to consistency. Templates eliminate the friction of starting from scratch.

## Post 2: The Trailer-Meat-CTA Framework
- **Date:** 2023 (ongoing pattern)
- **Summary:** Justin repeatedly posts about his three-part post structure. The Trailer (visible before "see more") breaks the scroll. The Meat delivers the promised value. The CTA drives comments (which extend reach).
- **Key Takeaway:** The first line should break the scroll, second line builds tension, last line before "see more" promises a payoff worth clicking for.

## Post 3: Solve One Expensive Problem Repeatedly
- **Date:** 2024
- **Summary:** Justin articulates his content philosophy: solve one expensive problem repeatedly. Every piece of content should orbit the core question: "How do you build attention or revenue on your own?" This keeps topic focus tight and audience aligned.
- **Key Takeaway:** Topic clarity beats content variety. Every post should serve one core positioning.

## Post 4: The Link-Edit Strategy
- **Date:** 2024-2025
- **Summary:** Justin shares his tactic of publishing posts without external links, letting them accumulate organic reach during the algorithm's initial scoring window (first 2-3 hours), then editing the post to add a business link or CTA afterward.
- **Key Takeaway:** LinkedIn penalizes posts with external links (~60% less reach). Publishing clean first, then editing in the link later, sidesteps this penalty.

## Post 5: 45 Minutes Every Morning
- **Date:** Various
- **Summary:** Justin has shared publicly that he spends ~45 minutes each morning responding to comments on his posts. He responds to approximately 75% of comments received.
- **Key Takeaway:** Engagement investment is non-negotiable. The algorithm rewards accounts that actively participate in conversations.

## Post 6: The 30-for-30 Method
- **Date:** 2024
- **Summary:** Justin describes his customer research practice: identify people in communities grappling with the problem his business addresses, offer 30 minutes of help in exchange for 30 minutes of them describing their problem in detail.
- **Key Takeaway:** Content ideas come from real customer conversations, not from guessing. This method fuels authentic, resonant content.

## Post 7: Visuals Now Dominate the Feed
- **Date:** 2026
- **Summary:** Justin notes that visuals now dominate the LinkedIn feed. Creators who aren't using carousels, infographics, or video are at a disadvantage. He continues to emphasize solving one specific problem repeatedly.
- **Key Takeaway:** Adapt to format shifts. Carousels and documents now get preferential algorithmic treatment.

## Patterns Identified
1. **Consistency over intensity** — Daily posting for years, not viral spikes
2. **System over inspiration** — Templates, batching, and workflows replace creative waiting
3. **Audience-first monetization** — Build trust through value, then monetize through aligned offers
4. **Topic discipline** — Stays inside solopreneurship/personal brand building; rarely strays
5. **Emotional resonance** — Writes to what people feel, not just what they think
