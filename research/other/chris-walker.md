# Chris Walker — Additional Sources

## Podcast: The Unlearn Podcast
- **Title:** The Revenue R&D Framework with Chris Walker
- **URL:** https://www.unlearnpodcast.com/episode/revenue-r-and-d-framework
- **Core insight:** Definitive episode on the **Revenue R&D framework** — his core methodology. Explains why demand gen splits into three phases (creation, capture, conversion), why MQL/SQL model is broken, self-reported attribution, restructuring marketing into a Revenue R&D function.

## Podcast: Revenue Vitals (formerly State of Demand Gen) #73
- **Title:** Demand Generation Essentials
- **URL:** https://www.anoopaulakh.com/blog/full-funnel-attribution-and-demand-generation-core-concepts-from-refine-labs
- **Core insight:** Codifies the **three phases of demand**: (1) Demand Creation (always-on education), (2) Demand Capture (converting existing intent), (3) Demand Conversion (pipeline to revenue). Explains why most companies over-invest in capture and under-invest in creation.

## Podcast: The Dave Gerhardt Show (Exit Five) #40/41
- **Title:** How to Make Marketing Bets
- **URL:** https://open.spotify.com/episode/458iLoOF76BE9HMlCXIkll
- **Core insight:** Scientific process for building, proving, and scaling marketing programs (Revenue R&D process). How to make marketing bets, measure demand creation separately from capture, think like an executive when allocating budget.
