# Adam Robinson — Additional Sources

## Podcast: SEEKERS
- **Title:** From 0 to $25M ARR Bootstrapped: Adam Robinson's Blueprint
- **URL:** https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/from-0-to-%2425m-arr-bootstrapped-adam-robinsons-blueprint/id1534096864?i=1000700644490
- **Core insight:** Most complete telling of his founder-led growth playbook. Explains why cold outreach is dying, the **"maximize humanity"** framework (being human in a world of AI content), audience-first launch strategy, and mistakes founders make automating sales too early.

## Podcast: Ahrefs Podcast
- **Title:** Turn LinkedIn Into a $30M ARR Growth Machine
- **URL:** https://podscan.fm/podcasts/ahrefs-podcast/episodes/turn-linkedin-into-a-30m-arr-growth-machine-adam-robinson-retentioncom-rb2b
- **Core insight:** Exact playbook for making LinkedIn the only customer acquisition channel to $30M ARR. Covers storytelling mechanics, "content-market fit," hook-and-template system for viral posts, why live webinars outperform other formats.

## Podcast: GTMnow #114
- **Title:** Audience-First GTM, Fueling Startup Growth via LinkedIn
- **URL:** https://gtmnow.com/gtm-114-audience-first-gtm-linkedin-growth-adam-robinson/
