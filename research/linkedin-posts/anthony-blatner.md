## Post 2: Thought Leader Ads vs Sponsored Content
- **Date:** March 17, 2025 (Exit Five Podcast #228)
- **Source:** https://exitfive.com/podcast/linkedin-ads-strategy-for-b2b-with-anthony-blatner/
- **Summary:** Thought Leader Ads generate 3-5x higher CTR than standard sponsored content. People are on LinkedIn to hear from people, not ads.
- **Key Takeaway:** Amplify people, not company pages. Thought Leader Ads are highest-ROI format.

## Post 3: Don't Use Default Settings
- **Date:** March 17, 2025 (Exit Five Podcast #228)
- **Source:** https://exitfive.com/podcast/linkedin-ads-strategy-for-b2b-with-anthony-blatner/
- **Summary:** LinkedIn's default "audience expansion" shows ads outside your target. For B2B, where precision is the value, this wastes budget.
- **Key Takeaway:** Turn off audience expansion. Tight targeting beats broad reach.

## Post 4: Cost Per Click Benchmarks
- **Date:** October 1, 2025 (Duct Tape Marketing Podcast)
- **Source:** https://ducttapemarketing.com/linkedin-ads-anthony-blatner/
- **Summary:** Average LinkedIn click costs $10-15 for standard company page ads. Thought Leader Ads with high engagement get $1-2 per click.
- **Key Takeaway:** High engagement = lower costs. The platform rewards content users enjoy.

## Post 5: Retargeting Strategy
- **Date:** October 1, 2025 (Duct Tape Marketing Podcast)
- **Source:** https://ducttapemarketing.com/linkedin-ads-anthony-blatner/
- **Summary:** Start with website retargeting (pixel your site) and company page visitor retargeting before going cold.
- **Key Takeaway:** Start with warm audiences. Don't go cold until you've maximized retargeting.
