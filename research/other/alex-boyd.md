# Alex Boyd — Additional Sources

## Podcast: Billion Dollar Backstory #96
- **Title:** Alex Boyd, Founder of Aware
- **URL:** https://www.billiondollarbackstory.com/episodes/96
- **Core insight:** Introduces the "LinkedIn is the introvert's conference" framework. Explains "being seen having conversations" — the real audience for comments is the quiet observers watching. Breaks down his 10-minute/day engagement system.

## Podcast: Life's a Pitch #22
- **Title:** B2B Social Media, SEO & the Future of LinkedIn
- **URL:** https://www.listennotes.com/podcasts/lifes-a-pitch-with/22-b2b-social-media-seo-the-_ZeXbrvnIGC/
- **Core insight:** Inside story of building Aware (1,000+ paying customers), LinkedIn shutting it down over unofficial API usage, and rebuilding on official APIs with DemandBird. Explains "stay in your lane" algorithm principle and why SEO and social media are merging.

## Podcast: Subscription Heroes #16
- **Title:** Content That Converts
- **URL:** https://churnkey.co/subscription-heroes/content-that-converts-real-talk-from-a-linkedin-pro-ft-alex-boyd/
